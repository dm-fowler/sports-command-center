How Scoring Works

Final score = Status + Team + Conference + Boosts - Penalties

Status: biggest base priority (LIVE vs UPCOMING vs FINAL).

Boosts: ranked matchups, close games, close-late games, tipoff timing.

Penalties: finals, blowouts, and low-interest live games.

Tune order: Status -> Boosts/Penalties -> Rules -> Team/Conference.
